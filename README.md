### About me 👋

I am third year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Notre Dame working with Dr. Diego Gomez-Zara. My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and human cognition in extended reality spaces. I focus on designing systems that respond to human needs, exploring how immersive environments and AI can augment the way we communicate, and collaborate.
